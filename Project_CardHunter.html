Card Hunter

Role: Game Designer
Genre: Turn-based Deckbuilder
Company: The Knights of Unity
Platform: PC

I've redesigned the entire in-game shop screen & UX flow. Card Hunter has been operating for a few years by the time,
so it was important to create a screen that would be easy to understand for both the old and new players alike.
I also used my analysis experience to help determine the roadmap for future content updates.
