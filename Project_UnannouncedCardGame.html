Unannounced Deckbuilder Game

Role: Lead Game Designer
Genre: Deckbuilder
Company: The Knights of Unity
Platform: PC

This project was the first time I took a leadership role, managing the team of 4-8 people to deliver a new game idea through the Concept and Pre-Production stage. We already had some initial requirements, but there was a lot of freedom in terms of core gameplay mechanics and core loops.
I distributed tasks between team members, provided feedback & design overview, communicated with other departments and kept the vision & direction of the project.
It was important for me to keep the balance between project needs and team needs, I also put a great focus towards early iteration and refinement of core gameplay ideas and loops.

- Lead a team of 4-8 people through Concept & Pre-Production stage of a new game title
- Maintained project vision/direction, handled results presentation & projected content needs (including needed Art assets)
- Managed task distribution, project direction & deadlines
