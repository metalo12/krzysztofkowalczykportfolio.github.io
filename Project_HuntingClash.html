Hunting Clash

Role: Live Ops / Game Designer
Genre: Simulation
Company: Ten Square Games
Platform: Mobile

In Hunting Clash, I've handled designing, balancing and analysing all Live-Ops game content, performance analysis and mentoring junior designers in our team.
Since this was a "rising star" project, a lot of focus was put into efficiency and proper performance analysis,
which is why i made an analysis framework that helped us with managing huge amounts of data and comparing different events / offers.

- Design & balance for all in-game Live-Ops content
- KPIs Analisys & performance reports
- Designed a custom KPI Analisys framework for performance reviews
- Mentored & assisted junior designers
