Traffic Puzzle

Role: Junior Live Ops / Game Designer
Genre: Match-3
Company: Picadilla
Platform: Mobile

For Traffic Puzzle, I've designed and balanced dozens of levels for Traffic Puzzle, creating a variety of challenges and a proper difficulty curve for our players. I've also designed and playtested the First Time User Experience for the game, including iterations based on player data and feedback. I've also designed new match-3 mechanics unique to the Traffic Puzzle and learned how to maintain project documentation.

- Designed dozens of puzzles with various difficulty
- Designed First-Time User Experience & new match-3 mechanics
- Content balance & documentation maintenance
