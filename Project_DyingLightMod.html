Dying Light Custom Mod

Role: Intern Level Designer
Genre: Action/Survival Horror
Company: Techland
Platform: PC

I created and iterated the layouts for 2 out of 3 major locations in the mod. All locations were based on real-life, thus requiring research and iteration to make them work with Dying Light mood and features. Working in a team of 5, I created a fully playable custom mod for Dying Light, under the supervision of people from Techland. Included in my contribution are enemy spawnpoints and all possible player routes. This internship was my first step into the gamedev world.

- Level blockout (2 out of 3 locations)
- Level Spawnpoints & possible player routes
