Let's Fish

Role: Live Ops / Game Designer
Genre: Simulation
Company: Ten Square Games
Platform: Mobile

In Let's Fish, my main resposibilities were centered around the Live-Ops side of the game. I've designed, balanced and analysed all content we were adding into the game, with heavy focus on in-game offers. Once i got accustomed with spreadsheets and tired of manually filling all live-ops content, I've taught myself Google Script and scripted a custom spreadsheet toolfor content generation which increased our efficiency 4 times(we measured!). I've also created performance reports and used a data-driven approach for game analysis.

- Design & balance for all in-game Live-Ops content
- KPIs Analisys & performance reports
- Scripted a custom spreadsheet tool for in-game events generation with Google Script
- Mentored & assisted junior designers
