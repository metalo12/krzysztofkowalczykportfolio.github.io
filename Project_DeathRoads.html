Death Roads: Tournament

Role: Game Designer
Genre: Turn-based Deckbuilder
Company: The Knights of Unity
Platform: PC

For Death Roads: Tournament, I have helped with the overworld map of a post-apocalyptic USA that shows player progression.
I have also worked on balancing the game to be more accessible to the less hardcore players, while maintaining the soul of the game.
This also included game tutorial and the UX of the main gameplay screen.

- Helped with creation of in-game map
- Helped with content balance and tutorial design
